About this place

Matilda Bay is a tranquil inlet on the Swan River, right next to the UWA campus in Crawley. It's one of those spots that's always worth visiting — whether you're a student taking a break between lectures or a Perth local looking for an evening stroll.

The sunset views here are absolutely stunning. The sky turns all shades of orange and pink, reflecting beautifully on the still water. On weekends you'll often see people picnicking on the grass, kayaking on the river, or just sitting quietly watching the boats drift past.

There's a small restaurant nearby and plenty of grassy areas for a casual sit-down. The walking path along the foreshore connects nicely to the broader UWA campus trail — great for a 30-minute relaxation loop between study sessions.
